Strategic Cost Management

1. VALUE ENGINEERING

Concept: Analyzing shoe components to replace high-cost parts with alternatives that provide equal function.

Action: Switched from premium imported leather to high-grade local tanned leather for the formal range, reducing costs by ₹100 per unit without visual quality loss.

2. KAIZEN COSTING

Concept: Continuous small improvements in production to shave off pennies per stage.

Action: Implemented a scrap-recycling program where leather off-cuts are used for accessories (tags/keychains), offsetting factory overheads by 5%.
